====== JMailHelper ======

This is an E-Mail helper class, provides static methods to perform various tasks relevant to the Joomla! e-mail routines. Like security methods to prevent [[http://www.securephpwiki.com/index.php/Email_Injection|Email Injection Attacks]]and validate that an email address is properly formated.

===== Methods =====
^ Method ^ Description ^
| [[JFile-copy|{{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/Method.png }}]] [[JMailHelper-cleanAddress|cleanAddress]] | Verify that an e-mail address does not have any extra headers.|
| [[JFile-copy|{{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/Method.png }}]] [[JMailHelper-cleanBodycleanBody|cleanBody]] | Clean any injected headers from the E-Mail body.|
| [[JFile-copy|{{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/Method.png }}]] [[JMailHelper-cleanSubject|cleanSubject]] | Clean any injected headers from the subject string.|
| [[JFile-copy|{{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/Method.png }}]] [[JMailHelper-isEmailAddress|isEmailAddress]] | Verify that the email adress is in valid format.|
