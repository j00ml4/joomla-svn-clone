This is an archive handling class.  In this context an archive is a file that contains other files which may or may not be compressed.  Examples are "tar" (uncompressed), "gz" (compressed) and "bz2" (best compression).  JArchive is a [[http://www.php.net/manual/en/language.oop5.static.php|static class]] and so its methods are available without needing an instantiation of the class.
