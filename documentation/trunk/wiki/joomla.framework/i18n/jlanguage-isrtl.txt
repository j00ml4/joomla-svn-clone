====== isRTL ======

| {{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/package.png?nolink}} [[references:joomla.framework|Joomla.Framework]] | {{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/package.png?nolink}} [[references:joomla.framework/#I18N|I18N]] | {{http://api.joomla.org/media/images/Class.png?nolink}}[[JLanguage|JLanguage]] | [[http://api.joomla.org/Joomla-Framework/I18N/JLanguage.html|JLanguage->isRTL]] | never | Work in progress |

Gets the RTL property (i.e. determines of the language uses a script that moves from right to left).

===== Syntax =====
boolean isRTL	( **$value** )

| **$value** | string | is not used. |

===== Examples =====
<code php|Example>
$german = JLanguage::getInstance( 'de-DE' );
$hebrew = JLanguage::getInstance( 'he-IL' );
echo "German " . ($german->isRTL() ? 'is' : 'is not') . " a right to left language.";
echo "Hebrew " . ($hebrew->isRTL() ? 'is' : 'is not') . " a right to left language.";
</code>
might produce:
<code php|Result>
German is not a right to left language.Hebrew is a right to left language.
</code>
